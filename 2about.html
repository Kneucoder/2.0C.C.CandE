What is endpoint protection and why is it important?

Endpoint protection secures all devices connected to your network (computers, laptops, mobile devices). Its a mechanism to prevent unauthorized access, malware, and data leaks.

What is a cybersecurity policy?

A documented set of rules and practices for employees and systems to follow, including password policies, data handling, and incident reporting.
